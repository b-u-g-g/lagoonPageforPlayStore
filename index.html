Delete Account & Data

How to request deletion of your Lagoon account and the data associated with it.

L
How do I request my account and data to be deleted?
You can delete your account directly inside the Lagoon app:

Open Lagoon → tap the wave icon in the app bar → Device Management → Delete My Account → Confirm.

Your request is processed immediately, no waiting period, no email required.
L
What data gets deleted when I do this?
Everything tied to your account is permanently removed, including:
  • Your email address and password
  • Your profile information (name, gender, date of birth)
  • Your education and skills data
  • Your location data
  • Your matches, messages, and project activity
L
Do you keep any of my data after I delete my account?
No. We don't retain any of your data after account deletion. Everything is permanently erased from our systems as soon as your request is confirmed.
L
Can I undo a deletion once I've confirmed it?
No. Deletion is immediate and irreversible once you confirm it in the app, so make sure you're ready before tapping confirm.
This page applies to the Lagoon app, developed by Lagoon. If you have any trouble deleting your account from within the app, you can reach out to us for assistance.
