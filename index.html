Delete Account & Data

How to request deletion of your Lagoon account and the data associated with it.

L
How do I request my account and data to be deleted?
You can delete your account directly inside the Lagoon app:

Open Lagoon → tap the wave icon in the app bar → Device Management → Delete My Account → Confirm.

Your request is processed immediately, no waiting period, no email required.
L
What data gets deleted when I do this?
Everything tied to your account is permanently removed, including:
  • Your email address and password
  • Your profile information (name, gender, date of birth)
  • Your education and skills data
  • Your location data
  • Your matches, messages, and project activity
L
Do you keep any of my data after I delete my account?
No. We don't retain any of your data after account deletion. Everything is permanently erased from our systems as soon as your request is confirmed.
L
Can I undo a deletion once I've confirmed it?
No. Deletion is immediate and irreversible once you confirm it in the app, so make sure you're ready before tapping confirm.
This page applies to the Lagoon app, developed by Lagoon. If you have any trouble deleting your account from within the app, you can reach out to us for assistance.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026 — how Lagoon collects, uses, and protects your data.

L
What information does Lagoon collect from me?
When you sign up and use Lagoon, we collect:
  • Account details — email address and password
  • Profile information — name, gender, date of birth
  • Education details — institution, degree, field of study, CGPA, graduation year
  • Skills and interests you add to get matched
  • Profile photo (if you choose to add one)
  • Location data, used to match you with nearby collaborators and projects
  • Basic device and app activity data, to keep the app working and secure
L
Why do you collect this information?
We use your information to:
  • Create and manage your Lagoon account
  • Match you with teammates, collaborators, and projects using our AI matching system
  • Show you relevant, nearby opportunities based on your location
  • Give you better, more personalized recommendations over time
That's it. We don't use your data for advertising, and we don't share it with anyone else.
L
Do you share my data with anyone else?
No. We do not share, sell, or rent your personal data to anyone. Your information is stored securely with us and used solely to give you better matches and recommendations within the app. We use Firebase Authentication to manage account sign-in, which is a standard, secure infrastructure service, not a third party we share your data with for their own use.
L
Is my data kept secure?
Yes. Your data is stored on secure servers and access is limited to what's needed to operate the app and generate your recommendations. We use encrypted connections to protect your data while it's transmitted between your device and our servers.
L
What control do I have over my data?
You can update your profile, skills, and education details in the app at any time. You can also delete your account and all associated data permanently, whenever you choose, from Device Management in the app. See the "Delete Account" tab for the exact steps.
L
Does Lagoon collect data from children?
Lagoon is intended for users who meet the minimum age requirement for creating an account, and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect data from children under this age.
L
Will this policy ever change?
We may update this policy from time to time as Lagoon grows. If we make significant changes, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
This privacy policy applies to the Lagoon app, developed by Lagoon. If you have questions about how your data is handled, you can reach out to us for assistance.

Child Safety Standards

Lagoon's published standards against child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

L
What is Lagoon's policy on child sexual abuse and exploitation?
Lagoon has zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) of any kind. This includes, without limitation:
  • Any content that sexually exploits, abuses, or endangers a child
  • Grooming, sextortion, or trafficking of a minor
  • Any attempt to use Lagoon to contact, solicit, or exploit a minor
  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) in any form, including AI-generated imagery
Lagoon is not directed at children and is intended only for users who meet our minimum age requirement to create an account. Any account found engaging in this kind of behavior is permanently banned.

What happens when a report is made?

  • Report confirmed CSAM to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) or the relevant local authority, in accordance with applicable child safety laws
